    About the game

    Embark on an exciting 2D action role-playing adventure with Wild Frontier: The Rescue. Immerse yourself in a pixelated world full of dangers and challenges as you face powerful enemies to rescue your son. With a real-time combat system and captivating pixel art visuals, this game offers an immersive and action-packed experience. Craft and upgrade your weapons in your desperate quest to be reunited with your loved one. Get ready to embark on an epic adventure in Wild Frontier: The Rescue!

    Your Mission

    Rescue your son from the enemy tribe, the bear tribe, they may have bears but you have your faithful companion a wolf

    The Plan

    Create and upgrade your wepons and face those who dared to separate you from your son and who want to prevent them from meeting again
